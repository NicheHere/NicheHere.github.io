Directly ahead of you is a crack in the stone wall, dirt gathers below the crack with what little light from the torches not reaching more then a few inches in.

To your right, a long deep and stony hallway, reminiscent of he walls you've walked past before, but the difference being the coffins and withering wood lining the walls on shelf like displays.

Finally to your left, and collapse of the stone and brick which leads into a deep caverm, ever vast, and ever empty.
